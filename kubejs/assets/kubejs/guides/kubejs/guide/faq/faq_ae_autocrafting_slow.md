# **Q: Why is my ae autocrafting so slow?**
**Q: Why is my ae autocrafting so slow?**

Short answer: you need more power, more crafting cpus, more assemblers, and to find the actual bottleneck.

* Add more AE2 energy cells to your network so crafts don't stall from lack of power.
* Build more and larger crafting CPU multiblocks; big and multiple CPUs let more complex crafts run in parallel.
* Put acceleration cards into your molecular assemblers so they craft faster.
* Build and use the Assembler Matrix and Quantum Computer multiblocks; they are designed for large, complex crafts.
* Place more machines directly adjacent to their pattern providers so AE2 doesn't waste time moving items around.
* Watch your crafts while they run and look where they pause or slow down; fix bottlenecks as soon as you add a new pattern, otherwise late‑game crafts become very hard to debug.
