# **Q: What’s the best armor/weapon?**
**Q: What’s the best armor/weapon?**

Short answer: Mid‑game: Mekasuit / AE2 Quantum Armor + Draconic gear; endgame: Avaritia.

* Pre‑endgame armor: Usually Mekasuit or AE2 Quantum Armor.
* You will also need the Draconic Evolution chestplate to fight the Chaos Guardian.
* Pre‑endgame weapons: Draconic Staff of Power plus Draconic Bow.
   * Upgrade the bow to Chaotic with your first chaos shards, so it can destroy the crystals and make future Chaos Guardian kills much easier.
* Endgame: Avaritia gear wins.
