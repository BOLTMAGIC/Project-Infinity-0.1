# **Q: Some blocks that imitate players don't work in claimed chunks (routers, item users, etc). why?**
**Q: Some blocks that imitate players don't work in claimed chunks (routers, item users, etc). why?**

Short answer: you need to allow fake players in the ftbchunks team settings.

* Open your inventory and click the "My Team" button at the top left.
* Click "Settings" at the top right to open team options.
* Set "Allow All Fake Players" to "True".
* Click "Accept" at the bottom to save the setting; fake‑player blocks should now work in your claimed chunks.
