# **Q: How do I get enough cu to complete the Infinity 9 research?**
**Q: How do I get enough cu to complete the Infinity 9 research?**
Short answer: You need all 8 Infinity Blocks, which requires 288 ultimate singularities.

In version 0.0.50.0 there are 8 Infinity Blocks in total.

To research Infinity 9 you need 288 ultimate singularities.

The exact numbers and steps are explained on the relevant quest page in the Crazy AE2 Addon quest line.
