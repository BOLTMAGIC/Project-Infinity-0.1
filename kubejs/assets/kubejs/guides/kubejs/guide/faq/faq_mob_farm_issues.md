# **Q: Why is my mob farm having all sorts of weird problems (mobs leaking, unpushable mobs, etc)?**
**Q: Why is my mob farm having all sorts of weird problems (mobs leaking, unpushable mobs, etc)?**

Short answer: some mods add special mobs with unusual rules, so classic mob farms are much harder to make foolproof.

* The pack includes mods (for example Born in Chaos) that add mobs with special movement and behavior rules.
* Many pushing issues can be fixed by combining fast vector plates with fans to move mobs more reliably.
* Most leakage can be reduced by making the walls thicker (one or two extra blocks), though some special mobs can still escape.
* It is very hard with this mod set to make a perfect natural spawn or cursed earth farm; consider alternatives like Hostile Neural Networks, Woot, Crazy AE2 Addons, or Apotheosis spawner‑based farms.
* WARNING: claim the chunks your farm is in (or disable mob griefing), otherwise parts of your farm can get blown up.
* As a periodic cleanup option, you can add a mob crusher with a large working area, run it on a pulse, and control it with a slow timer (for example a hovering hourglass).
