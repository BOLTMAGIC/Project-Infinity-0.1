# **Q: why is pipez getting removed, and what are better pipe mods?**
**Q: why is pipez getting removed, and what are better pipe mods?**

Short answer: pipez is too laggy; recipes are removed and items hidden, and there are several better alternatives.

* Pipez is being removed because it causes too much lag/TPS issues in large setups.
* In version 0.0.48.2, all Pipez recipes are removed and its items are hidden to make it easier to fully remove in a later update.
* Good alternatives include:
  * SFM (Super Factory Manager)
  * Integrated Dynamics
  * LaserIO
  * XNet
  * Modular Routers
  * AE2 / Refined Storage cables
  * Curvy Pipes (recommended, very TPS‑friendly)
  * Logistic Networks (recommended, very TPS‑friendly)
  * EnderIO
  * Mekanism
