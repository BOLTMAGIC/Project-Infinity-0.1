# **Q: How do i share emc with other people?**
**Q: How do i share emc with other people?**

Short answer: use the team command from projecte.

* Use the command:
  >/team_projecte invite PLAYER_NAME
* Replace PLAYER_NAME with the player you want to share EMC with.
* Courtesy of Dursty79.
