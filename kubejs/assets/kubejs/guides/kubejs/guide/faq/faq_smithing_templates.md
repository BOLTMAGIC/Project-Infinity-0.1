# **Q: Where do I get smithing templates?**

**Q: Where do I get smithing templates?**

Short answer: Each template comes from a specific structure or dimension.

* **Netherite**: Large bastion remnants with rectangular lava pools. Check the chests at the bottom of the big central room.
* **Allthemodium**: Ancient Cities in the Lost Cities dimension. Rare in chests or from brushing suspicious clay.
* **Vibranium**: Same bastion type as Netherite. Brush suspicious soul sand; make sure there is a block underneath so it doesn’t fall.
* **Unobtanium**: Dungeon structures in the Other dimension. Use the Explorer’s Compass to find the huge underground dungeon; templates rarely appear in chests in “library” rooms.
* **Alfsteel**: Kill evokers and witches with an elementium sword.
