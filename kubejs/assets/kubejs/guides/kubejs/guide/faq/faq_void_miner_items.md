# **Q: How do I deal with the flood of items from my maxed‑out void miner?**
**Q: How do I deal with the flood of items from my maxed‑out void miner?**

Short answer: Use advanced logistics and automation mods to buffer, route and process items.

* Use Curvy Pipes to build a large, high‑throughput item network.
* Super Factory Manager (SFM) can handle complex routing, filtering and parallelization.
* Integrated Dynamics (ID) is great for logic‑based sorting and smart item networks.
* Logistic Networks help push items automatically to storage, processing or trash.
