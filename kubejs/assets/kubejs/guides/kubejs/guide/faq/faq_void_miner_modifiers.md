# **Q: What's the optimal modifier combo for the void miner tiers?**
**Q: What's the optimal modifier combo for the void miner tiers?**

Short answer: each tier has one or two best configurations; some require resources from that tier before you can use them.

Note: miners with a second configuration listed cannot use that setup from the start, because it needs resources generated by that miner.

###### <Color id="red">Tier 1 – Rubetine miner</Color> ######
>* No modifier slots.
>* Output: 1 item every 1000 ticks.

###### <Color color="#ff9500">Tier 2 – Aurantium miner</Color> ######
>* 2 Aurantium item modifiers.
>* Output: 2 items every 900 ticks.
>* Citrinetine item modifiers do not improve this; still 2 items every 900 ticks.

###### <Color id="yellow">Tier 3 – Citrinetine miner</Color> ######
>* 4 Citrinetine item modifiers:
>  * Output: 8 items every 800 ticks.
>* 4 Verdium item modifiers:
>  * Output: 10 items every 800 ticks.

###### <Color id="green">Tier 4 – Verdium miner</Color> ######
>* 6 Verdium item modifiers:
>  * Output: 34 items every 700 ticks.
>* 6 Azurine item modifiers:
>  * Output: 47 items every 700 ticks.

###### <Color id="aqua">Tier 5 – Azurine miner</Color> ######
>* 8 Azurine item modifiers:
>  * Output: 169 items every 600 ticks.
>* 8 Caerium item modifiers:
>  * Output: 256 items every 600 ticks.

###### <Color id="blue">Tier 6 – Caerium miner</Color> ######
>* 10 Caerium item modifiers:
>  * Output: 1024 items every 500 ticks (needs T2 storage upgrade).
>* 1 Aurantium item modifier + 1 Azurine item modifier + 8 Amethystine item modifiers:
>  * Output: 1149 items every 500 ticks (needs T2 storage upgrade).

###### <Color color="#b300ff">Tier 7 – Amethystine miner</Color> ######
>* 1 Aurantium item modifier + 1 Azurine item modifier + 8 Amethystine item modifiers + 2 Amethystine speed modifiers:
>  * Output: 1149 items every 243 ticks (needs T2 storage upgrade).
>* 2 Amethystine item modifiers + 7 Rosarium item modifiers + 3 Rosarium speed modifiers:
>  * Output: 1100 items every 175 ticks (needs T2 storage upgrade).

###### <Color color="#ff00d4">Tier 8 – Rosarium miner</Color> ######
>* 2 Amethystine item modifiers + 7 Rosarium item modifiers + 7 Rosarium speed modifiers:
>  * Output: 1100 items every 43 ticks (needs T2 storage upgrade).
>* 1 Rubetine item modifier + 8 Ultimate item modifiers + 7 Ultimate speed modifiers:
>  * Output: 2288 items every 24 ticks (needs T3 storage upgrade).

###### Tier 9 – Ultimate miner ######
>* 1 Verdium item modifier + 9 Caerium item modifiers + 1 Ultimate item modifier + 13 Ultimate speed modifiers:
>  * Output: 2304 items every tick (needs T3 storage upgrade).
